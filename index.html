Hi, I'm Tony Hawk!

I´m, a professional skateboarder and entrepreneur. Known as the "Birdman," I revolutionized skateboarding with my innovative tricks, including the 900, which I landed in 1999. Throughout my career, I have won numerous championships and helped bring skateboarding into the mainstream. Beyond skating, I founded the Tony Hawk Foundation to support skateparks in underserved communities and created the Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater video game series, inspiring generations of skaters worldwide.
